# **SaaS Revenue Intelligence & Churn Analysis**

An **End-to-End SaaS Analytics** project that analyzes customer retention, revenue performance, churn behavior, and business growth using **SQL** and **Power BI**. The project provides actionable insights into customer health, subscription trends, and revenue intelligence through interactive dashboards.

---

# **Project Overview**

This project simulates a real-world SaaS analytics workflow by combining SQL-based data analysis with Power BI dashboards to monitor business performance and customer behavior.

---

# **Features**

* Revenue Intelligence
* Customer Churn Analysis
* Customer Segmentation
* Customer Health Monitoring
* Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) Analysis
* Customer Retention Analytics
* Executive Power BI Dashboard

# **Dataset**

The project uses two datasets:

* **subscriptions.csv** – Customer subscriptions, plans, churn status, acquisition channels, feature adoption, support activity, and customer health metrics.
* **monthly_revenue.csv** – Monthly revenue, customer growth, churn metrics, acquisition performance, and SaaS KPIs.

---

# **Key Insights**

* Enterprise customers generated the highest revenue.
* Annual subscriptions showed stronger customer retention.
* Low product adoption increased churn risk.
* High support dependency strongly correlated with churn.
* Customer health scoring effectively identified high-risk customers.
* Acquisition channel quality directly influenced long-term retention.
